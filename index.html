About Me

Background

I am a researcher in image quality assessment (IQA) and multimodal AI, currently completing my M.Sc. at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) in Xi'an, China, under the supervision of Associate Professor Xu Li.

My research develops no-reference IQA frameworks using multivariate Gaussian (MVG) distributional modeling combined with handcrafted natural scene statistics and deep semantic features from vision-language models like CLIP and LLaVA.
