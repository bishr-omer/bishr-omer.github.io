Investigated **YOLOv10** architecture advancements and optimized the model for real-time inference, achieving improved mAP/latency tradeoffs on benchmark datasets. Implemented and benchmarked **EDSR** and **LapSRN** super-resolution networks using PyTorch and OpenCV, analyzing perceptual and distortion performance on standard SR evaluation datasets.
